=======
iPad Promo App Installation
=======

1. Save the email text to notes.
2. Delete the EGI Promo App from your device.
3. Open safari and go to egi.utah.edu/promoapp/
4. Type in the password: EGIPromo2016
5. Tap on Install EGI Promo App
6. Close safari and open the EGI Promo App that was just installed
7. Go to Settings, enter in your email text, and tap save.
8. Tap on Update Promotional Materials
